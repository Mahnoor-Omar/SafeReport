âš Online Harassment

What is it? Cyberbullying, stalking, threats, or unwanted contact through digital platforms.

How to protect yourself:

  • Keep personal information private
  • Use privacy settings on social media
  • Block and report harassers immediately
  • Save evidence (screenshots, messages)
  • Don't engage with trolls or harassers

ğŸ’³Online Fraud

What is it? Scams designed to steal money, personal information, or identity through fake websites, emails, or calls.

How to protect yourself:

  • Verify website URLs before entering data
  • Never share OTPs or passwords
  • Be suspicious of "too good to be true" offers
  • Use secure payment methods
  • Check bank statements regularly

ğŸš«Digital Abuse

What is it? Using technology to control, intimidate, or harm someone, including sharing private content without consent.

How to protect yourself:

  • Be cautious about sharing intimate content
  • Regularly check who has access to your accounts
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Trust your instincts about suspicious behavior
  • Seek help from trusted friends or authorities

ğŸ”’Cybersecurity Threats

What is it? Malware, phishing, data breaches, and other attacks targeting your devices and information.

How to protect yourself:

  • Keep software and apps updated
  • Use antivirus protection
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be careful with public Wi-Fi
  • Backup important data regularly

Privacy Policy

At SafeReport, your privacy is our top priority. Here's how we handle your information:

  • No personal data is required to submit a report.
  • Anonymous submissions are fully supported.
  • Uploaded files are used only for reviewing reports and are not stored permanently.
  • We do not use cookies, trackers, or collect browse data.
  • Your data is never shared with third parties.
  • All submitted cases are carefully monitored and reviewed by our professional team to ensure authenticity.
  • Each piece of evidence is thoroughly examined, and only verified cases are forwarded to relevant authorities such as the FIA.
